FAQ
What is SmartResidency and who is it for?
SmartResidency is an IoT-based mobile platform for residential complexes. It's designed for residents, building management staff, security guards, and administrators of modern apartment complexes. Everyone uses the same app — just with role-based access to the features they need.
How does the smart barrier and parking control work?
Guards can open and close barriers directly from the app, with a full event log of all entries and exits. IoT sensors monitor each parking spot in real time — residents see whether their assigned space is occupied and admins see the full lot overview. Permanent and guest spots are managed separately.
Is the mobile app available on both iOS and Android?
Yes. SmartResidency is built with Flutter, which compiles natively to both iOS and Android from a single codebase. This means residents and staff can use the app on any modern smartphone without separate development cycles.
How do I submit a maintenance request?
Open the app, go to Service Requests, and tap "Create Request." Describe the issue, attach photos if needed, and select the category (plumbing, electrical, cleaning, etc.). Your request is routed to the appropriate staff member and you receive live status updates — New, In Progress, or Done.
How do IoT sensors work in the building?
Sensors installed throughout the complex stream live events to our cloud backend. Admins see all sensor alerts across the building. Verified residents see the status of sensors in their entrance. Any unresolved event triggers push notifications via Firebase, so security personnel are alerted immediately — even when the app is closed.
How do I get SmartResidency deployed for my complex?
Fill out the contact form on this page or email us at info@smartresidency.io. Our team will schedule an on-site consultation, assess your complex's infrastructure, and walk you through the deployment process — from IoT sensor placement to staff onboarding.
