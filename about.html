About Us

Built to transform how residential complexes operate

SmartResidency is an IoT-based platform that unites residents, staff, and building management into one seamless digital ecosystem — making modern living smarter, safer, and more efficient.


Founded as a graduation project by a team of three specialists in Astana, Kazakhstan, our platform has grown into a fully functional mobile solution with real IoT integration, live monitoring, and automated access control.

What drives us

Our Mission

To digitize and simplify residential complex management through smart technology — giving residents real-time control over their home environment and giving managers visibility they've never had before.

Our Vision

A Kazakhstan where every residential complex is digitally connected — with transparent management, safe automated access control, and a thriving resident community built on real-time communication.

Our Values

Innovation, security, and simplicity guide everything we build. Great technology should feel invisible — just working, reliably, every single day, without friction for any user role.

Our Story

A diploma project with a real vision

SmartResidency is a graduation project created by a team of three students at a Kazakhstani university. The goal was simple: design and build a complete IoT-based solution for managing a modern residential complex.

We developed the mobile app, cloud backend, IoT sensor integration, and this website entirely from scratch — as a functional prototype demonstrating what smart residential management could look like.

Technology

Flutter & Dart

Cross-platform mobile app for iOS and Android from a single codebase. Material 3 design with role-based navigation for residents, guards, staff, and admins.

IoT Sensors

Real-time sensor data for motion detection, environmental monitoring, and security alerts. Sensors stream live events to the cloud backend via SSE.

Cloud Backend

Scalable REST API with Server-Sent Events for real-time push updates. Role-based access control, document verification workflows, and parking management.

Firebase

Push notifications via Firebase Cloud Messaging for instant security alerts. Residents receive real-time alerts for sensor events even when the app is closed.
