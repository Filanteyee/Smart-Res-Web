Services

Everything your complex needs in one app

SmartResidency brings together six core modules — from guest access and barrier control to real-time sensor monitoring and community voting. Select a service below to explore what it does.

01
Guest Access & Passes

Let your guests in without leaving your apartment

Residents generate a digital QR pass directly from the app. Guards scan it at the entrance — no calls, no waiting, no paper.

  • Generate a QR pass for any guest in seconds
  • Set a custom validity period for each pass
  • Guards validate passes instantly via the app
  • Full history of all guest visits
  • Manage multiple active passes simultaneously
02
Service Requests

Report issues and track them in real time

Residents submit maintenance requests with photos and a description. The request goes straight to the right staff member — residents follow the status without making a single call.

  • 7 categories: plumbing, electrical, cleaning, garbage, security, intercom, elevator
  • Attach photos as evidence directly from the phone
  • Live status: New → In Progress → Done
  • Admin assigns and manages all open requests
  • Staff see their personal task queue in the app
03
Barrier & Parking Control

Smart access and real-time parking management

Guards control the barrier from the app and view the full log of vehicle events. IoT sensors on each spot update occupancy in real time — residents see their spot's status without going outside.

  • Guards open and close the barrier from the app
  • Full timestamped log of all vehicle movements
  • IoT sensors detect occupancy of each parking spot
  • Permanent spots assigned to specific residents
  • Residents get an alert if their spot is taken
  • Admin sees the full lot occupancy at a glance
04
Communication Tools

Keep every resident informed, instantly

Building management publishes announcements that reach all residents as push notifications. Security alerts are broadcast in real time — no notice boards, no missed messages.

  • Management publishes announcements from the admin panel
  • Residents receive instant push notifications via Firebase
  • Dedicated notifications feed with read/unread status
  • Security and sensor alerts delivered in real time
  • Full notification history available in the app
05
Resident Voting

Give residents a voice in complex decisions

Building management creates polls on community matters. Residents vote directly in the app and results are visible to everyone in real time — no meetings required.

  • Management creates polls with custom options and deadlines
  • Residents vote from the app in one tap
  • Real-time vote counts visible to all participants
  • Results stored for transparency and accountability
  • Voting periods close automatically at the deadline
06
Analytics & Monitoring

Full visibility over your complex in real time

The admin dashboard aggregates data from all IoT sensors, parking spots, and service requests in one place — so administrators can see the health of the entire building at a glance.

  • Real-time status of all IoT sensors across the complex
  • Online/offline tracking with instant failure alerts
  • Parking lot occupancy with spot-level detail
  • Service request statistics and response times
  • Full security event history with resolution status
  • Per-entrance sensor grouping for large complexes
